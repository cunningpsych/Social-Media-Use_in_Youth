\subsection{Procedure}\label{procedure}

The Monitoring the Future Main Study is a survey that is presented annually to eighth, tenth, and twelfth graders to observe how answers may change over time. The survey results are split into two data sets, one for eighth and tenth grade participants and one for twelfth grade participants. For the sake of this study, items were pulled from the 2023 8th- and 10th grade survey codebook to create scales of social media use, impulsivity, depression, and anxiety.
